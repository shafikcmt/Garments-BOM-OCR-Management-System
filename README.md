# Garments BOM / OCR Management System## Project Overview**Garments BOM / OCR Management System** is a centralized digital platform designed for garment manufacturing operations. It helps manage the full business workflow from **order creation to shipment**, replacing scattered manual Excel-based tracking with one organized and controlled system.In many garment operations, teams depend on 40-50 separate Excel files to manage orders, BOM, purchase requirements, commercial documents, store updates, accounts activities, and shipment tracking. This creates duplicate work, slow communication, version confusion, and limited visibility across departments.This system brings those processes into a single workspace where multiple departments can work together with clear responsibility, better control, and real-time operational visibility.---## Purpose of the SystemThe main purpose of this system is to simplify and control the garment order lifecycle by connecting key departments in one platform.It supports business teams in managing:- Order information- Buyer and style details- Bill of Materials (BOM)- OCR-based document/data processing- Purchase and PO-related workflow- Store and inventory coordination- Commercial documentation support- Accounts-related control- Shipment progress tracking- Approval and role-based activity control- Dashboard-based monitoring---## Business Problem It SolvesGarments production and export operations usually involve multiple departments working on the same order using different Excel files. This often causes:- Repeated manual data entry- High risk of mistakes- Delayed communication between teams- Missing or outdated files- Difficulty tracking who changed what- Poor visibility for management- Slow reporting and decision-making- Complex follow-up from order to shipmentThis system reduces those issues by creating one controlled digital workflow where information can be entered, reviewed, updated, and tracked from a single source.---## Key Departments SupportedThe system is designed for cross-department collaboration, including:### Merchandising TeamManages order details, style information, BOM preparation, buyer requirements, and order progress follow-up.### Commercial TeamSupports export/import-related documentation flow, commercial data handling, and shipment-related coordination.### Store TeamTracks material receiving, availability, issue status, and inventory-related updates.### Accounts TeamSupports cost, payment, PO, and finance-related monitoring based on approved workflow data.### Management / Admin TeamControls user roles, permissions, workflow access, approval visibility, dashboards, and reporting views.---## Core Features### Centralized Order ManagementManage garment orders in one place with structured information, status tracking, and department-wise visibility.### BOM ManagementPrepare, review, and control Bill of Materials data for styles, items, colors, sizes, quantities, and related requirements.### OCR-Based Data AssistanceUse OCR-supported document handling to reduce manual typing and improve document-to-system data entry efficiency.### PO and Purchase ControlManage purchase-related information, PO generation workflow, and approval-based control for responsible teams.### Role-Based Access ControlEach department receives access based on responsibility. Admin can control who can view, create, update, approve, or manage specific parts of the system.### Workflow MonitoringTrack process status from order stage to shipment stage with better visibility and accountability.### Dashboard and ReportsProvide management-friendly dashboards for order progress, pending tasks, PO status, shipment updates, and department-wise activities.### History and Change VisibilityMaintain better control over updates, changes, and process movement so teams can understand the latest status clearly.---## High-Level Workflow```textOrder Entry   ↓BOM Preparation   ↓Review / Approval   ↓Purchase / PO Process   ↓Material Receiving / Store Update   ↓Commercial & Accounts Coordination   ↓Shipment Preparation   ↓Shipment Completion   ↓Management Review & Reporting```---## Why This System Is ValuableThis platform helps garment businesses move from manual Excel dependency to a structured digital process.Key benefits include:- Reduces dependency on many separate Excel files- Improves inter-department communication- Reduces manual mistakes and duplicate work- Gives management better visibility- Improves order follow-up and accountability- Speeds up purchase, store, and shipment coordination- Helps teams work from one controlled source of information- Makes the overall workflow easier, faster, and more organized---## Designed for Real Garments OperationsThe system is built around practical garment business workflows where merchandising, commercial, store, accounts, and management teams work together on the same order. It is suitable for companies that want to digitize their internal order-to-shipment process and reduce operational complexity.---## Client-Focused Summary**Garments BOM / OCR Management System** is a business management solution for garment factories and export-oriented companies. It helps control the full workflow from order to shipment, supports multiple departments, reduces manual Excel work, and provides a more reliable way to manage production-related business information.This system is intended for organizations that want a modern, centralized, and role-controlled platform to manage garment order operations more efficiently.---## Ownership and Usage NoticeThis system is a proprietary business solution.
