# Business Workflow

## High-Level Workflow

```text
Order Entry
↓
BOM Preparation
↓
Review / Approval
↓
Purchase / PO Process
↓
Material Receiving / Store Update
↓
Commercial & Accounts Coordination
↓
Shipment Preparation
↓
Shipment Completion
↓
Management Review & Reporting
```

## Department Responsibilities

### Merchandising

- Create and maintain order information
- Manage buyer, style, color, size, item, and BOM details
- Prepare purchase requirement data
- Follow order progress
- Coordinate with purchase/store/commercial/accounts

### Management / Admin

- Control users, roles, and permissions
- Monitor dashboards and reports
- Review pending workflow
- Check department-wise progress
- Control approval visibility

### Store

- Track material receiving
- Update stock/material availability
- Manage issue/receive status
- Support purchase and production follow-up

### Commercial

- Manage import/export/shipment-related document support
- Coordinate with merchandising and accounts
- Track shipment readiness and document progress

### Accounts

- Monitor cost, payment, PO, and finance-related data
- Support approval/payment tracking
- Coordinate with purchase/commercial flow

## Workflow Improvement Principle

Every feature should make the process easier, faster, and more visible than the old Excel-based system.

Before adding a new screen or field, ask:

- Which department will use it?
- What Excel/manual work will it reduce?
- What management visibility will it improve?
- Does it affect role access?
- Does it need PDF/Excel output?
